# 7K Law Prep: Research Plan & Competitive Strategy

> **Objective:** To validate and refine the "AIR 1" Master Blueprint by analyzing the current competitive landscape, identifying critical gaps, and proposing a winning feature set that blends content depth, AI personalization, and user-centric design.

## 2. SWOT Analysis for 7K Law Prep

Based on the landscape, here is where we stand:

### **Strengths (Internal)**
*   **Tech-First DNA:** We are building with Next.js, AI (Gemini), and modern web tech, allowing for superior UX compared to legacy coaching apps.
*   **Agility:** We can ship features like "AI Courtroom" and "Visual Law" faster than incumbents.
*   **Holistic Vision:** We are the *only* ones explicitly combining "Performance" (Mocks) + "Knowledge" (Notes) + "Mindset" (Wellness).

### **Weaknesses (Internal)**
*   **Content Volume:** We currently lack the massive repository of 10,000+ questions that Testbook has.
*   **Brand Trust:** As a new player, we don't yet have the "AIR 1" alumni network of LegalEdge.
*   **Offline Parity:** Our current web-first approach needs robust offline capabilities to match native apps.

### **Opportunities (External)**
*   **"Hybrid Fatigue":** Students are tired of rigid Zoom schedules. They want *guided self-paced* learning.
*   **AI Expectation:** Users now *expect* AI to explain *why* an answer is wrong, not just show the solution.
*   **Micro-Transactions:** Students are willing to pay small amounts (₹50-₹100) for specific value (e.g., "Unlock 5 Mocks") rather than huge upfront fees.
*   **Gamification Gap:** No serious competitor has fully nailed "Duolingo for Law" – streaks, leagues, and social accountability.

### **Threats (External)**
*   **Copycats:** Established players adding "AI Chat" buttons (though likely superficial wrappers).
*   **Exam Pattern Changes:** Sudden shifts in CLAT pattern rendering static content obsolete.

## 3. Feature Gap & Differentiation Strategy

To win, we must not just match but *leapfrog*.

| Feature Category | Standard (Competitors) | 7K Law Prep "AIR 1" Standard |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mock Tests** | Static PDF/Web interface, simple score. | **Predictive Rank Engine:** AI predicts AIR based on difficulty + peer performance. **"Mistake Genome":** Auto-tags errors (e.g., "You suck at Torts when tired"). |
| **Notes** | Static PDFs, maybe highlighted. | **"Visual Law Studio":** Interactive flowcharts (Mermaid) for case laws. **Linked Notes:** Click a section → see related MCQs instantly. |

| **Planning** | Generic PDF schedule. | **Dynamic Blueprint:** Auto-adjusts daily goals based on yesterday's performance and available hours. |
| **Community** | Noisy Telegram groups. | **"War Rooms":** Focused, small-group squads with shared goals and leaderboards. |
| **Settings/UX** | Basic toggle, logout. | **"Control Center":** Granular notification tuning, "Zen Mode" (hide metrics), Offline content manager. |

## 4. The "Best in the World" Settings Page

The current Settings page is functional but basic. To be a premium "OS for Law Aspirants," it needs to be a **Control Center**.

### **Proposed Structure:**

1.  **Profile & Identity**
    *   Avatar customization (unlockable badges).
    *   "Aspirant Type" selector (School Topper, Dropper, etc.) to tune the AI recommendation engine.
    *   Target Exam Year (CLAT 2026, 2027).

2.  **App Preferences (The "Zen" Factor)**
    *   **Theme:** System / Light / Dark / *Midnight (OLED)*.
    *   **Focus Mode:** Toggle to hide "Rank" and "Percentile" if they cause anxiety (Mental Health feature).
    *   **Font Size:** Accessibility slider for reading long passages.

3.  **Notifications (Granular Control)**
    *   "Daily Knowledge Drop" (Morning).
    *   "Study Reminder" (Custom time).
    *   "Streak Saver" (Urgent nudge).
    *   *Disable all marketing/upsell notifications.*

4.  **Data & Offline (Crucial for India)**
    *   **"Offline Library":** Manage downloaded mocks/notes. Show storage usage.
    *   **"Smart Sync":** Auto-download next week's plan on Wi-Fi.

5.  **Subscription & Billing**
    *   Clear view of active plan.
    *   One-tap "Top-up" for specific features (e.g., "Buy 5 more Mocks").

6.  **Support & Feedback**
    *   "Report Content Error" (Direct line to content team).
    *   "Chat with Support".

---

## 5. Actionable Recommendations

1.  **Immediate:** Upgrade the **Settings Page** to include "Focus Mode" and "Aspirant Profile". This signals we care about *them*, not just the test.
2.  **Short-term:** Implement **"Smartkeeda-style" Analytics** but with better UI. The "Suggestion Mode" (skip/attempt) is a high-value feature we should copy and improve.
3.  **differentiation:** Double down on **Gamification (Qudoo style)** but keep it serious. "Daily Legal Wordle" or "Case Law Bracket" to build daily habits.
4.  **Content:** We cannot beat Testbook on volume yet. We must beat them on **Curation & Linkage**. Every question must be a "gold standard" question with deep explanations.

This plan positions 7K Law Prep not just as a tool, but as a *competitive advantage*.
